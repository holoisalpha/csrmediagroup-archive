The power of creator marketing lies in enabling your brand to align its message to a creators personal brand (their personality), gaining access to an already engaged audience that aligns with your brand. When thisis done properly, marketing messages come across as organic and genuine. This is much more effective than posting a banner ad or releasing a commercial.

Trust
