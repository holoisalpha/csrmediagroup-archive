COMPANY OVERVIEW

CSR media group is a digital media agency that helps the world's largest brands activate creators to drive growth. Our team is led by mission driven experts in the fields of brand and program development, influencer marketing, content marketing, advertising and communications. Top brands like Canadian Tire, Visa, Ford, MLSE, NHL and others partner with CSR media group to achieve their critical marketing objectives.

As an organization we don’t see the relationships we develop through the conventional client-vendor lens. We build partnerships that produce long term results and deliver a measured increase in brand awareness, social engagement and consumer trust.

We work with our brand partners to understand their unique marketing objectives and develop custom strategies. Our dedicated team manages campaigns from end-to-end, including ideation, strategy development, creator identification, outreach and management, content development and distribution, tracking and reporting on all all key performance indicators.
