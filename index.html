CASE STUDIES

  • Canadian Tire          2016 RIO Olympics

    Objective

    Throughout every Olympic games, there are moments of courage and sportsmanship that transcend sport. Our task was to engage Canadians in a large scale conversation about those moments occurring daily throughout the RIO Olympic games. 

    Results

    Trending Hashtag throughout Opening Ceremony 
    133 creators (33% over delivery)
    5,295 pieces of content (660% over delivery)
    143 million+ impressions  
    $200k+ raised for Jumpstart 

  • VISA + NHL MOVEMBER

    Objective

    Leveraging Visa's sponsorship of the NHL our campaign offered Movember fundraisers the opportunity to enlist the support of various NHL stars in their fundraising efforts through customizable videos, memes, and audio clips that could be shared on social. 

    Results

    305 pieces of content created (>100% more than projected) 
    58 creators activated (90% more than projected)
    54 million + impressions 
    Over $15,000 raised for Movember by creators
