Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

CSR Media Group Inc. ("CSR Media Group," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of our clients, creators, website visitors, and partners. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or engage with our services.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • Fill out a contact form or inquiry on our website
  • Register to join our creator network
  • Communicate with us via email or other channels
  • Participate in a campaign managed by CSR Media Group

This information may include your name, email address, phone number, company name, website URL, social media handles, and any other information you choose to provide.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and communicate with you about our services
  • Match brands with appropriate creators for marketing campaigns
  • Manage and execute creator marketing campaigns
  • Analyze campaign performance and provide reporting to our clients
  • Improve our website and services
  • Comply with legal obligations

Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

  • Brand Partners: When you are a creator participating in a campaign, relevant profile information may be shared with the brand client to facilitate the partnership.
  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or servicing you.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, regulation, or legal process.

Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Social Media and Creator Data

As a creator marketing agency, we may collect and analyze publicly available social media data including follower counts, engagement rates, content performance, and audience demographics. This data is used to match creators with brand campaigns and to measure campaign effectiveness.

Cookies

Our website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience. You can set your browser to refuse cookies, but some features of the website may not function properly.

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites and social media platforms. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites and encourage you to review their privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or port your personal data. To exercise these rights, please contact us at info@csrmediagroup.com.
